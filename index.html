About Me


If want to know things about me that are beyond my resume, you've come to the right place. I won't try to explain my personality in a few words. People are too complex for that. Instead, I'll tell you what I enjoy doing.


I enjoy programming, but I also enjoy doing many other things. In fact, what I love the most about programming is how it can apply to most things we do in life. Among other things, I enjoy watching movies and TV shows, playing boardgames, and reading fiction. Some of these hobbies inspired many of the projects I've worked on, don't forget to take a look at those!


One of my biggest passion since high school has been playing volleyball. I'm currently a member and secretary of the men's volleyball club at my school. I also enjoy playing with people of all levels in RIT's intramural leagues. I'm mostly an indoor volleyball player, but I recently started playing beach volleyball as well. This opened up yet another way to stay active and meet amazing people who help me become a better teamplayer.


I've grown a particular interest towards the social aspects of coding; I'm interested in how people come together to solve a problem through programming. This interest drove me into competitive coding sites likes CodeFights (See my profile). The same interest also takes me to Hackathons around me, where I worked on some of the projects showcased on this website


I developed other small hobbies in the past 3 years through different classes and clubs. These include things likes playing the piano and gardening. I'm no expert in either of those things, but I enjoy doing them and I have a feeling they'll become a bigger part of my life in the future.
